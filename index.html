About Us


A non-profit organization started in 2003, with just 12 members and
the only motive was to display talents in all forms, shapes and sizes
without discrimination.

Being in the 21st century art could easily become underappreciated
In order to avoid that we have formed the "Art-Appreciation Society".

With over a million users* worldwide,now creators,sponsors,students,representatives
all have a chance to understand and value art in all of its different
forms be it painting,sculpting,dancing,singing the list could go on
forever.
